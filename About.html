Excel Global College is an institution with a vision of providing the best quality of education at most affordable fees to each and everyone. The college provides training for professional qualifications and computer based courses.

Excel Global College is an ABE accredited college, authorized ACCA computer based exam centre, Microsoft I.T. academy, Certiport exam centre and Tally partners. The college provides training in various professional courses like ABE, ACCA and CPA (FAQ). Excel Global College is an ABE accredited college and an ACCA Gold Approved Learning Provider.

Excel Global College also provides I.T. courses like Tally, Quick books, Sage, computer packages, programming, graphic designing, web designing, Microsoft office specialist (MOS) and other Microsoft certified courses. Microsoft certified courses like MOS are accepted and recognized internationally and the students get certification from Microsoft after clearing the exams. The college also provides training in languages like English, IELTS and TOEFL.
